About

Hi, I'm Jun, a master's student and researcher at Korea University advised by Dr. Heuiseok Lim, with a deep commitment to making Large Language Models transparent, reliable, and aligned.

I focus on LLM Evaluation, building clear, reproducible frameworks to measure capabilities, find failures, and track progress across shifts and task mixes. I also work on Mechanistic Interpretability to reverse‑engineer transformer behavior behind reasoning, coding, and language.

I study the principles of deep neural networks and connect theory to practice. My goal is to help build capable, responsible AI systems that align with human values.

I work across evaluation, interpretability, safety, and reasoning. Understanding how models work is key to unlocking capability and reducing risk. If you’re exploring these areas, I’d love to connect.
